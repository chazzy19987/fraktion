// How it works

DEAD SIMPLE.

Four steps from cluttered to clear — and your stuff is always just a tap away.

01
📦

We Drop Off Your Bins

We deliver heavy-duty 102L bins right to your door. Each one gets a unique ID and is yours to fill.

02
🏷️

Label in the App

Tag each bin with names, descriptions, and photos. "Christmas stuff," "baby gear," "camping equipment" — find anything fast.

03
🏢

We Store It

Request a pickup and we come get your filled bins. Safe, climate-controlled storage — at a fraction of the cost of a locker.

04
🚚

Deliver On Demand

Need your Halloween decorations? Tap "Deliver" in the app. We'll have it at your door — fast. Return it just as easily.
